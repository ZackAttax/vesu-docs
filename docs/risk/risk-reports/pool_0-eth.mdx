## Summary

Ether (ETH) is the base asset of the Ethereum blockchain. 
This report is prepared for an ERC20 token reflecting ETH bridged to Starknet. 
The canonical bridge for ETH on Starknet is StarkGate, a non-custodial bridging protocol. 
Over $1.5B-worth of ETH has been bridged to Starknet to date.

More information can be found under the following links:

- [StarkScan](https://starkscan.co/token/0x049d36570d4e46f48e99674bd3fcc84644ddd6b96f7c741b1562b82f9e004dc7)
- [StarkGate](https://starkgate.starknet.io/)
